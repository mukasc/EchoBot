# Scrybe Quill - FAQ

**Answers to everything you need to know about Scrybe Quill.**

### What is Scrybe Quill?
Scrybe Quill is a recapping tool designed for tabletop role-playing game players. It allows you to upload recordings of your TTRPG sessions, which are then transcribed, summarized into engaging recaps, and narrated using text-to-speech (TTS) technology.

You can customize your recaps with thematic background music and choose from a variety of TTS voices to narrate your recap in an engaging and immersive way. Scrybe Quill aims to preserve the essence of your adventures in a shareable format that is meant to help kick off the adventure at the beginning of your next session.

### How long does it take to generate a recap?
The generation time can vary depending on the length of your recording and number of words spoken. On average, it takes no more than 5 minutes for the text recap and notes to complete, with the audio and video recaps taking up to an additional 5 more minutes to generate.

### What audio format should my recording be?
Most common audio file formats are supported including MP3, WAV, and M4A. To ensure compatibility and efficient processing, please ensure your recordings are in one of these supported formats before uploading. Lossy formats such as MP3 are recommended to reduce upload and processing time. There is a maximum file size limit of 1.5GB per upload.

### What quality do my recordings need to be?
High audio recording quality is not necessary! Scrybe Quill is capable of generating recaps from recordings that use even the worst of microphones. Record on your phone, laptop, or any other device you have on hand, and Scrybe Quill will take care of the rest.

### What if there's background music or sound in my recordings?
Scrybe Quill's transcription tech is top notch and is capable of transcribing what was said despite background music or other ambient noise. Don't worry about it!

### What if my players take a break or go off-topic during the recording?
Scrybe Quill is designed to ignore all off-topic discussions and to focus solely on the main content of your game session. Scrybe Quill will only consider the plot-related conversations when generating the recap.

### Is Scrybe Quill limited to specific TTRPG systems?
Scrybe Quill is TTRPG agnostic, meaning it is designed to work with any tabletop role-playing game system, not just popular ones like Dungeons & Dragons or Pathfinder. Whether you're playing an indie RPG or a well-known game, Scrybe Quill can help you generate recaps for your sessions, preserving the essence of your adventure no matter the system.

### What languages does Scrybe Quill support?
Scrybe Quill has full multilingual support. This means you can upload a recording in any language and the text, audio, and video recaps will be produced with fluent writing and pronunciation in the same language. All narrators can speak any of the supported languages.

To use multilingual support, make sure to select "Multilingual" under the Language dropdown in the advanced options.

### What are Ink Drops, and how do I acquire them?
Ink Drops are Scrybe Quill's currency. Each droplet corresponds to one full recap generation. You can acquire Ink Drops by purchasing them through the website or for free during special promotions. Scrybe Quill also has a referral program that rewards you with Ink Drops for inviting friends to join Scrybe Quill.

### Can I edit the recap made by Scrybe Quill?
Yes! To manually fix any details, make sure to check the "Manually edit recap before generating" checkbox under the advanced options. Check back after a few minutes once your text recap is ready to be edited, make any changes you see fit, and then confirm your changes to continue generating the audio and video recaps.

### Are there any copyright issues with the background music provided?
All background music available on Scrybe Quill is either copyright free under a variant of the Creative Commons license or is specifically licensed to let you use it for any purpose as long it is paired with the narration produced by Scrybe Quill.

If you wish to use your recap for commercial purposes, you are completely free to do so, but you may need to attribute the original music composer. For detailed information on each track's source and license, please refer to the Music Credit page for details.

### Is my data safe with Scrybe Quill?
Scrybe Quill takes your privacy and data security seriously. All personal data is stored securely using Google Firebase and your session transcriptions and recaps are not publicly accessible unless you share them using link sharing.

Scrybe Quill does not store your recordings long term. They are automatically deleted immediately after the transcription is complete. Make sure to keep a backup of your recordings if you wish to keep them for your own future use.

Please refer to Scrybe Quill's Privacy Policy for more information on how your data is handled.

### How does Scrybe Quill use AI?
Scrybe Quill uses AI for transcribing recordings, condensing transcriptions into recaps, and narrating the recap using text-to-speech (TTS).

* **Transcription** is handled by OpenAI's Whisper, one of the most accurate speech-to-text models available.
* **Recap generation** is powered by OpenAI's GPT-5 (optionally Gemini 2.5 Pro). It condenses the full transcription into a concise summary, corrects spelling mistakes, and fixes transcription errors. It also takes notes on NPCs, items, locations, factions, quests, and player characters from the session.
* **Text-to-speech (TTS)** narration is prepared using ElevenLabs. Any voices based on real people were cloned with explicit permission, and those voice actors earn a commission whenever their voice is used.
* **No AI-generated music or art.** All background music is either licensed or commissioned from real artists. In support of creatives, Scrybe Quill does not use AI-generated images.

More details on Scrybe Quill's AI use can be found in the Privacy Policy.

### Who can access my TTRPG session recordings and recaps?
For now, only you can access your recordings and recaps. Recap sharing is currently not available, however you are free to download the text, audio, and video files created by Scrybe Quill and share them as you see fit.

### I'm experiencing technical issues with Scrybe Quill. How can I get help?
If you encounter any technical problems or have questions about using Scrybe Quill, please reach out via email to info@scrybequill.com. If the problem concerns a partially failed recap generation, you will be refunded the Ink Drop used for the generation.

Your feedback and suggestions are greatly appreciated! To help improve Scrybe Quill, please send an email to info@scrybequill.com. Your input is invaluable in helping make Scrybe Quill magical.
