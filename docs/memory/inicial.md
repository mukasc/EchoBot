# [SECTION: HOW IT WORKS]
**1. Record Your Game**
Record your game sessions using your phone, laptop, or Discord bots like Craig. Don't worry about background noise or off-topic chatter; our AI is designed to handle it.

**2. Upload Your Audio**
Drag and drop your recording. We support most common audio formats with a maximum file size of 1.5GB and sessions up to 8 hours long.

**3. Review and Edit**
In about 5 minutes, you'll receive a detailed text recap and session notes. Use our editor and "Find & Replace" tool to make sure every name and detail is perfect.

**4. Narrate and Share**
Choose a thematic narrator and background music. In another 5 minutes, you'll have a fully narrated audio and video recap ready to share with your party.
